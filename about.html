Meet Our Pâtissiers

François Perret

François Perret is an accomplished and innovative pastry chef with a wealth of experience in the world of patisserie. With over 15 years of expertise, François has honed his skills working in renowned patisseries and luxury hotels around the globe. His passion for the art of pastry led him to create exquisite and visually stunning creations that delight both the eye and the palate. François brings a modern and creative approach to traditional patisserie, infusing his creations with unique flavors and textures that leave a lasting impression. His dedication to quality and attention to detail make him a true master of his craft.

Julien Dugourd

Julien Dugourd is a highly skilled and talented patissier known for his expertise in crafting delectable cakes and bread. With a background in traditional French patisserie, Julien has spent years perfecting his techniques and mastering the art of creating indulgent desserts. His journey has taken him through some of the finest bakeries and culinary institutions, where he has refined his skills under the guidance of renowned pastry chefs. Julien's creations are a harmonious blend of flavors and textures, showcasing his commitment to using premium ingredients and traditional baking methods. His passion for his craft is evident in every mouthwatering bite.
